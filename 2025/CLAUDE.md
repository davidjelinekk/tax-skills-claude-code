# Tax Year 2025

Returns for the tax year ending December 31, 2025.
Entity returns due **March 15, 2026**. Individual return due **April 15, 2026**.

## Key Law Changes for TY2025

The One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA), signed July 4, 2025:
- QBI deduction (Section 199A): **permanent**
- 100% bonus depreciation: **permanently restored** (property acquired after 1/19/2025)
- SALT cap: raised to **$40,000** (phase-down above $500K AGI)
- Section 179: raised to **$2,500,000**
- Child Tax Credit: **$2,200/child**, ACTC $1,700 refundable
- Standard deduction: increased $750 (single) / $1,500 (MFJ)
- New deductions: tips ($25K), overtime ($12.5K/$25K), auto loan interest ($10K)
