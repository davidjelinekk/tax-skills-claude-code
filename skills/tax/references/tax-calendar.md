# Tax Calendar — Federal and Illinois Deadlines

## Annual Filing Deadlines

### Entity Returns (Due FIRST — K-1s needed for individual returns)

| Return | Original Due Date | Extended Due Date | Late Penalty |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Form 1065 (Partnership/LLC) | March 15 | September 15 | $235/partner/month (max 12 mo) |
| Form 1120-S (S-Corp) | March 15 | September 15 | $235/shareholder/month (max 12 mo) |
| IL-1065 (IL Partnership) | March 15 | September 15 | IL penalties apply |
| IL-1120-ST (IL S-Corp) | March 15 | September 15 | IL penalties apply |

### Individual Returns

| Return | Original Due Date | Extended Due Date | Late Penalty |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Form 1040 (Individual) | April 15 | October 15 | 5%/month of unpaid tax (max 25%) |
| IL-1040 (IL Individual) | April 15 | October 15 | IL penalties apply |
| Form 1120 (C-Corp) | April 15 | October 15 | Varies |

### Information Returns

| Form | Due Date | Penalty |
|------|----------|---------|
| 1099-NEC (Contractor payments) | January 31 | $60-$660/form |
| 1099-MISC (Other payments) | February 28 (paper) / March 31 (e-file) | $60-$660/form |
| 1099-K (Payment processors) | January 31 | $60-$660/form |
| W-2 (Employee wages) | January 31 | $60-$660/form |

### Illinois (IL-1040-ES) — Same quarterly schedule as federal

| Quarter | Due Date |
|---------|----------|
| Q1 | April 15 |
| Q2 | June 15 |
| Q3 | September 15 |
| Q4 | January 15 (next year) |

### Safe Harbor Rules (Avoid Underpayment Penalty)
Pay at least the LESSER of:
- **90%** of current year's tax liability, OR
- **100%** of prior year's tax liability (**110%** if prior year AGI > $150,000 / $75,000 MFS)

### No Penalty If:
- Total tax owed is less than **$1,000** after withholding/credits (federal)
- Total IL tax owed is less than **$500** after withholding/credits

### Payment Methods
- **Federal**: IRS Direct Pay (irs.gov/payments), EFTPS, or mail Form 1040-ES voucher
- **Illinois**: MyTax Illinois portal (mytax.illinois.gov) or mail IL-1040-ES voucher

## Holiday/Weekend Adjustments
When a deadline falls on a weekend or federal holiday, it moves to the next business day. When April 15 falls on a weekend or Emancipation Day (DC holiday), the deadline shifts to the next business day (sometimes April 16, 17, or 18).
