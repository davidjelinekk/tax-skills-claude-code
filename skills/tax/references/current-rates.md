### Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT)
- Additional **3.8%** on investment income for AGI over $200,000 (single) / $250,000 (MFJ)
- NOT indexed for inflation

### Collectibles Rate
- **28%** (includes NFTs if classified as collectibles)

### Section 1250 Recapture
- **25%** on unrecaptured Section 1250 gain (depreciation on real property)
