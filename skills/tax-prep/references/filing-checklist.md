# Tax Filing Document Checklist

Comprehensive document gathering checklist organized by entity type and income source.

## Personal (Form 1040) Documents

### Identity & Filing Status
- [ ] Social Security numbers for all household members
- [ ] Dates of birth for all dependents
- [ ] Prior year tax return (federal and IL)
- [ ] IRS Identity Protection PINs (if assigned)
- [ ] Marriage certificate (if newly married)
- [ ] Divorce decree (if newly divorced — affects filing status)
- [ ] Death certificate (if widowed)

### Wage & Salary Income
- [ ] W-2s from all employers
- [ ] W-2G (gambling winnings)
- [ ] Final pay stubs (to verify W-2 accuracy)

### Self-Employment / Business Income
- [ ] 1099-NEC (nonemployee compensation received)
- [ ] 1099-K (payment processor income — PayPal, Stripe, Venmo, Square)
- [ ] 1099-MISC (other income received)
- [ ] Business income/revenue records (invoices, sales reports)
- [ ] Business expense receipts organized by category
- [ ] Vehicle mileage log (date, destination, purpose, miles)
- [ ] Home office measurements and related home expense records
- [ ] Inventory records (if applicable)
- [ ] List of all contractors paid $600+ (for 1099-NEC you issued)

### Investment & Retirement Income
- [ ] 1099-B (stock/bond/crypto sales — from brokerages)
- [ ] 1099-DIV (dividends)
- [ ] 1099-INT (interest income)
- [ ] 1099-OID (original issue discount)
- [ ] 1099-R (retirement distributions, pension income)
- [ ] SSA-1099 (Social Security benefits)
- [ ] 1099-DA (digital asset/crypto transactions)
- [ ] Crypto exchange annual tax reports
- [ ] Brokerage consolidated tax statements
- [ ] K-1 forms from all partnerships, S-Corps, trusts
- [ ] Cost basis records for assets sold (especially if not reported on 1099-B)

### Rental Income
- [ ] Rental income records (lease agreements, rent received)
- [ ] Rental expense receipts (repairs, maintenance, insurance, management)
- [ ] Property depreciation schedules from prior year
- [ ] 1099-MISC received for rental income
- [ ] Property tax bills paid
- [ ] Mortgage interest (Form 1098)
- [ ] Time log if claiming Real Estate Professional Status

### Deductions — Itemized (Schedule A)
- [ ] Mortgage interest (Form 1098)
- [ ] Property tax bills paid on personal residence
- [ ] State and local income taxes paid (IL estimated payments, prior year balance)
- [ ] Charitable donation receipts and acknowledgment letters
- [ ] Charitable donation records for cash gifts $250+
- [ ] Non-cash charitable donation records (Form 8283 for items >$500)
- [ ] Medical/dental expense records (if >7.5% of AGI)
- [ ] Casualty/theft loss records (presidentially declared disaster only)

### Credits & Other
- [ ] Childcare expenses: provider name, address, EIN, amounts paid
- [ ] Education expenses (Form 1098-T — tuition statement)
- [ ] Student loan interest (Form 1098-E)
- [ ] Educator expenses receipts (if K-12 teacher, up to $300)
- [ ] EV purchase records (Form 15400 — clean vehicle credit)
- [ ] Energy-efficient home improvement receipts (Form 5695)
- [ ] Foreign tax paid records (if claiming foreign tax credit)
- [ ] Adoption expense records
- [ ] Health insurance records (Form 1095-A for marketplace, 1095-B/C)

### Retirement & Savings
- [ ] IRA contribution records (Form 5498)
- [ ] 401(k)/403(b) contribution records (shown on W-2)
- [ ] Solo 401(k) contribution records
- [ ] SEP IRA contribution records
- [ ] HSA contributions/distributions (Form 5498-SA, 1099-SA)
- [ ] 529 plan contribution records (for IL state deduction)

### Estimated Tax Payments
- [ ] Federal estimated tax payment records (amounts and dates for all 4 quarters)
- [ ] IL estimated tax payment records (amounts and dates)
- [ ] Prior year overpayment applied to current year (from prior return)
- [ ] PTE tax payments made by entities

## Partnership / Multi-Member LLC (Form 1065) Documents

### Entity Information
- [ ] EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- [ ] Operating Agreement (for ownership percentages, allocation provisions)
- [ ] Prior year Form 1065 and K-1s
- [ ] Partner information: names, SSNs/EINs, addresses, ownership %
- [ ] New partner agreements or partner withdrawals during the year

### Income
- [ ] Business income records (invoices, sales reports)
- [ ] 1099s received by the partnership
- [ ] Interest and dividend income
- [ ] Gain/loss on asset sales

### Expenses
- [ ] All business expense receipts organized by category
- [ ] Payroll records and W-2s issued to employees
- [ ] 1099-NECs issued to contractors
- [ ] Rent, utilities, insurance records
- [ ] Depreciation schedules from prior year
- [ ] New asset purchases (date acquired, cost, description)
- [ ] Loan interest records
- [ ] Professional fees (legal, accounting)

### IL-Specific
- [ ] IL replacement tax calculation (1.5% of net income)
- [ ] PTE tax election decision and estimated payments made
- [ ] IL Annual Report confirmation ($75 fee)

### Partner Basis Tracking
- [ ] Each partner's beginning-of-year tax basis
- [ ] Capital contributions during the year
- [ ] Distributions during the year
- [ ] Partner loans to/from partnership
- [ ] At-risk amounts per partner

## S-Corporation (Form 1120-S) Documents

### Entity Information
- [ ] EIN
- [ ] Form 2553 (S-Corp election confirmation)
- [ ] Articles of Incorporation/Organization
- [ ] Prior year Form 1120-S and K-1s
- [ ] Shareholder information: names, SSNs, addresses, ownership %

### Payroll (Critical for S-Corps)
- [ ] W-2s for shareholder-employees (verify reasonable compensation)
- [ ] W-2s for other employees
- [ ] Quarterly payroll tax returns (Form 941)
- [ ] Annual FUTA return (Form 940)
- [ ] State payroll tax records (IL)
- [ ] Payroll service annual summary
- [ ] Reasonable compensation analysis documentation

### Income & Expenses
- [ ] Same categories as partnership above
- [ ] Officer compensation detail
- [ ] Health insurance premiums paid for >2% shareholders (report on W-2)
- [ ] Retirement plan contributions (employer match, SEP, etc.)
- [ ] Shareholder distributions records

### Shareholder Basis Tracking
- [ ] Form 7203 (S-Corp Shareholder Basis) from prior year
- [ ] Stock basis: + income, + contributions, - distributions, - losses
- [ ] Debt basis: loans from shareholder to corporation (requires promissory note)

## Year-Over-Year Comparison Checklist

Before filing, compare current year to prior year:
- [ ] Total income — significant change? Explain why.
- [ ] Each major expense category — any large swings?
- [ ] New deductions or credits being claimed
- [ ] Any items that changed category or treatment
- [ ] Estimated tax payments — sufficient for safe harbor?
- [ ] K-1 income matches between entity and individual returns
