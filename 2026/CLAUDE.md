## Key Changes from TY2025

- Charitable deduction floor: 0.5% of AGI (new under OBBBA, effective 2026+)
- SALT cap: $40,400 (1% increase from 2025)
- SS wage base: $184,500 (up from $176,100)
- Trump Accounts: Can be funded starting July 4, 2026
- Solo 401(k) super catch-up: $11,250 (ages 60-63)
- IRA limits: $7,500 (up from $7,000)
